QUESTIONS:
1. What Nmap flag did you use to find the specific software versions running on these ports?
Answer: I used the -sV flag, which enables service version detection by probing open ports to determine the 
specific software and version number.

2. Why is it dangerous to run an intense scan (like -A) on a fragile legacy system?
Answer: 
Intense scans send a high volume of complex packets that can overwhelm older hardware or poorly written services, 
potentially causing the system to crash or stop responding.
